
[ENGLISH]

CLIENT / VEHICLE:

- A customer may have more than one vehicle

- A vehicle can have 1 customer

CLIENT / CONTRACT:

- A customer may have more than one contract

- A contract can have 1 client

CONTRACT / VEHICLE:

- A contract may have 1 vehicle

- A vehicle may have 1 contract
